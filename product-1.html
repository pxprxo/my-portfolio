Spilt Bill Application Design

Wish there was an easier way to split the bills when you're out with friends?


Overview

Splitting bills with friends can be a headache, especially when you're out shopping, dining, or traveling together. This app takes away the stress and awkwardness by helping everyone know exactly what they owe; no more guessing, no more confusion. Just scan a photo of the bill or type it in, choose who’s involved, and the app does the math for you. You can even adjust who paid what and keep track of everything in one place. It’s simple, clear, and makes group spending way less complicated.



Defining the Problem

Here are some of the common frustrations people face,

    • Trying to remember who ordered the extra side of fries or the pricier cocktail can lead to confusion and disagreements.

    • Sometimes everyone wants to split everything equally, even if some people had less.
    It can feel unfair and lead to resentment.

    • Keeping track of who owes whom can become a messy web, and those verbal promises to pay back often get lost in the shuffle.

    • Planning a trip with friends is exciting, but figuring out shared expenses like accommodation, transport, and activities can quickly become overwhelming.

Objective

Making Sharing Costs Simple, Fair, and Enjoyable!

The main aim of this app is to take away the frustration of splitting bills and managing group finances. Here’s what you’re setting out to do,

    Easy Bill Division
    To provide a super simple method for users to divide expenses accurately, whether by scanning a photo of the receipt or entering items themselves.

    Just and Equitable Sharing
    To allow users to customize how costs are divided, ensuring everyone pays their fair share based on what they used or agreed to.

    Clear and Understandable Records
    To offer a transparent view of who owes what, eliminating any uncertainty or confusion.

    Straightforward Payment Tracking
    To help users keep tabs on who has paid their part, making settling up quick and convenient.

    Simplified Group Finance Planning
    To offer tools that assist friends in planning and managing shared funds for trips or other group activities.
